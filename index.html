Listening Evaluation — Participant Portal

Before you begin

1

Put on your headphones and disable any EQ or sound-enhancement apps.

2

Find a quiet room with no interruptions. The full study takes about 15–20 minutes.

3

Complete the three tests below in order. Each test ends with an automatic CSV file download — please keep these files safe.

4

After finishing all three tests, email your three CSV files to the address at the bottom of this page.

1 Sound Quality Rating For musical participants

Rate three piano audio conditions across six quality attributes. Requires at least 2 years of music training.

⏱ ~20–30 min  |  8 trials (2 practice, 6 rated)
Open Test 1 →

2 Noise Threshold Test

Identify which mystery recording (X) matches one of the labelled examples (A, B or C). Background noise is gradually added across blocks.

⏱ ~5 min  |  15 trials (5 blocks: clean + 4 noise levels)
Open Test 2 →

3 Bandwidth Discrimination Test

Identify which mystery recording (X) matches one of the labelled examples (A, B or C). The audio bandwidth is narrowed across blocks.

⏱ ~5 min  |  15 trials (3 blocks: full bandwidth, low-pass 2 kHz, mid-range 300–8 kHz)
Open Test 3 →
